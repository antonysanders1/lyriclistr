About

Welcome to LyricListr:
a one page web app that allows developing songwriters to share their new lyrics snippets they're working on and get feedback from other writers.

Instructions

Browsing
Select a song to view from the provided list. When you're done, browse another.

Creating
Select the 'Create New Song' button and fill out the new song form. Once your done click 'finish'. Change your mind and want to return to browsing? Click 'Cancel'.

Commenting
Afer selecting a song to review, you can leave some helpful feedback or a comment for the witer to read. Have Fun and thank you for using!
